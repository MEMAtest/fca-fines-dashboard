## Content Strategy Notes
- **Monthly roundups** (1st of each month): Target "fca fines [month] 2026" long-tail keywords. These are time-sensitive and capture search traffic from compliance professionals checking monthly updates.
- **Topical pieces** (mid-month): Evergreen content targeting sector-specific and regulatory-theme keywords. These build domain authority and attract broader search traffic.
- **All posts** link to the FCA Fines Dashboard with CTA buttons, driving traffic from blog → interactive tool.
- **Internal linking**: Each monthly roundup should reference relevant topical pieces and vice versa.
- **Yearly review cards** (2013-2025) are auto-generated from `yearlyArticles` in `blogArticles.ts` — no calendar entries needed.
